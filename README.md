ğŸ“± How It Works
1. Start on the Landing Page
Watch our cinematic intro, search for your destination, or explore featured locations.
2. Search Flights
Enter your departure city, destination, and travel dates. Choose your travel class and number of passengers. Hit search and browse available flights.
3. Book Your Flight
Select your preferred flight, enter passenger details (passport info, contact details), and confirm your booking. Get a confirmation with a unique booking ID.
4. Find a Hotel
Search hotels in your destination. View ratings, read reviews, and select the perfect place to stay. Enter guest details and complete your reservation.

5. View Your Itinerary
See your complete trip overview with flight and hotel details. Explore your destination on an interactive map. Discover local attractions and plan activities.
6. Claim Offers
Login to access exclusive travel deals. Claim discounts on flights, hotels, and packages. Each offer can only be claimed once per user.
